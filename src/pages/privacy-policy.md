**Effective Date:** November 22, 2025

At Retro Gaming Verse (the "Blog"), operated by Steven, your privacy is important to us.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information
when you visit retrogamingverse.com or any other media form related to the Blog
(collectively, the “Sites”).

Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access the site.

## Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the Sites includes:

- **Personal Data:** Personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, and IP address, which may be collected when you submit comments, contact us via email, or subscribe to updates (if implemented later).
- **Derivative Data:** Information our servers automatically collect when you access the Sites, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and pages viewed.
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you use the Sites, including pages visited and time spent.

We do not collect financial information or sensitive personal data unless required for specific features.

## Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you allows us to provide a smooth and efficient experience.  
Specifically, we may use information collected via the Sites to:

- Create and manage your account (if applicable).

- Compile anonymous statistical data to improve the Blog.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.  
We may share data with service providers (such as hosting or analytics tools) under strict confidentiality.
